About DevFest

DevFest is the largest annual gathering of developers, software engineers, designers and technology enthusiasts across 571 Google Developer Groups in over 103 countries in the world to connect, learn and build problem solving technology solutions that affect the world around them. This event is designed to expose participants various technologies that will help to kick-start their software development career or start up. .

This event will bring together 700+ Technology enthusiasts from 4 states, 6+ Tertiary Institutions in the South West region of Nigeria
10+ Speakers including Top Nigerian Tech. Influencers across the World
Featuring: Android, Web, Firebase, Cloud Technologies including Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Startup Advice
